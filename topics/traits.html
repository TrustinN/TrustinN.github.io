Traits

Though I have taken the Myers-Briggs Personality Test, I have forgotten my letters.

There are several traits that I do see in myself, however. I enjoy learning in-depth and gaining a deep understanding of what I am working with. This is half the reason that I chose to study math. As for the other, I love some of the tricks, results, and way of thinking that comes from it. Solving problems simply triggers my curiosity.

I enjoy being alone sometimes. Although it may bother some people, I don't mind being away from other people for long periods of time. It's like I have an internal friend, and their name is brain. Maybe I am delusional.

I can sometimes get too focused on a task. Such as with gaming, yet I have found other fun things to do. It also happens with other hobbies such as studying math the whole day or playing the piano for extended hours. I just enjoy doing whatever I feel like in the moment or what I envision myself doing in the future. Perhaps it is not the best habit though. I still regret the week I spent before finals week configuring my neovim editor (I talk about this in my Interests section).
